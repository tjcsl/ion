Accessing your school files from home

  • Download and install FileZilla Client.
  • Open the application, and press the icon in the top left corner to open Server Manager.
  • In the dialog, press New Site. For the host, enter tj03.local.tjhsst.edu or, to access your Computer Systems Lab filesystem, remote.tjhsst.edu. Select SFTP along with your TJ credentials, and press Connect.
  • Accept the Unknown host key warning if it appears.
  • Navigate through the folder structure to find the files that you want. To access your local network drive, choose the correct folder for your class year. To access the common R drive, choose R.
  • To upload a file, ensure that you have navigated to the correct locations on both the local (left) and remote (right) sides. Then, right click the file or folder on the left pane and select Upload.
