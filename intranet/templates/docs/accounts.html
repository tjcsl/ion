CSL Account Documentation

Introduction

The TJHSST Computer Systems Lab (also known as the CSL or SysLab) maintains various services to support the students & staff of TJHSST. Starting this year, setting your password for Ion, Webmail, and other CSL services will be through a different process. This page describes how to manage your CSL account and answers any questions about these changes.

If you have any questions with regard to your CSL account or are having trouble logging into your CSL account, please contact the Sysadmins at accounts@tjhsst.edu.


Setting/Resetting Passwords

You can reset your password by visiting https://resetter.tjhsst.edu. Once you click authenticate, you will prompted to login to your FCPS Google Apps for Education account. Do NOT login to your personal Google account.

After logging in, you will be redirected to the password reset page where you can reset your password.


Frequently Asked Questions - Students

What has changed from previous years?

Starting this year, there is only one place to set your password for Ion & other CSL services: https://resetter.tjhsst.edu. Changing your password on any TJ Windows computer NO LONGER allows you to login to Ion & other CSL services.

What is my TJ username? How is this different from my FCPS student ID?

Your TJ username allows you to interact with CSL services.

For TJ students, this is composed of your graduation year, first initial, first seven characters of your last names, and, if necessary, an additional numeric identifier. For example, if you graduate in 2023 and your name is John Doe, your TJ username is 2023jdoe. If you graduate in 2023 and your name is Alice Longestlastnameintheworld, your TJ username is 2023alongest.

If someone else shares the first initial and first seven letters of the last name, a sequential number will be added based on the student's placement in alphabetic order. For the purposes of this username, all dashes and spaces in your first and last names are ignored.

If you login to https://resetter.tjhsst.edu with your FCPS Google Apps for Education account, it will display your TJ username in the first line.

Your FCPS username is managed at the district-level, is used to authenticate to your FCPS Google Apps for Education, Blackboard, and SIS accounts. and is composed of just seven digits.


Frequently Asked Questions - Staff

What is my TJ username?

For TJ staff, your TJ username is the same as your FCPS username. Your FCPS username is the username you use to login to your FCPS Google Apps for Education, Blackboard, or SIS account.

Can I reset CSL passwords for my students?

Please direct students to this page if they need assistance.

Why can't I login to Webmail as a staff member?

Webmail accounts are only granted to students. Please contact accounts@tjhsst.edu if you have special needs.


Frequently Asked Questions - General

What services does the Computer Systems Lab account allow access to?

The Computer Systems Lab maintains the student intranet (Ion), student mail accounts (TJ Webmail), and website management application (Director). In addition, the CSL supports TJ's various computer science courses, the Computer Systems Senior Research Lab, and other critical infrastructure for the school.

What are the password requirements for CSL accounts?

CSL passwords must be at least eighth characters long. They must also contain at least two character classes (out of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters).

Can I use the same password for my FCPS account and CSL account?

Yes, as long as they meet the requirements set by FCPS and the CSL.
