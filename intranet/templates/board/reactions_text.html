Introducing the fast new way to collaborate at TJ: Ion Connect

Ion Connect was created with the dream of allowing students to communicate and collaborate with each other as effortlessly as possible. Our research team embarked on an extensive, year-long study to answer one overarching question: How can students foster their desire to embark on frictionless and instantaneous methods of communication in our inherently competitive TJ environment? The answer, of course, was simple: internet memes, the societal backbone of 21st century Internet users. And, as an equally important component in today's social media-driven world, the ability to effortlessly communicate your opinions through emoji. By combining modern society's two most vital constructs, we are proud to have truly created a social media platform for the modern age, exclusively available through the TJ Intranet.
