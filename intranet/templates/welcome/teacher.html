How to Navigate

Intranet is fully optimized for small screens. On your phone, tablet, or other mobile device, simply visit ion.tjhsst.edu. To access navigation items and the search bar, press the icon on the top left of the page next to the Intranet logo.

For detailed instructions on using the Ion teacher interface, press the Help button on the left-side navigation, or ask 8th Period Office Coordinator Catherine Forrester.

About Intranet

Development on Intranet version 3 began in early 2013 and, after more than two years of work, was first used during the 2015-2016 school year. While the application has been released, development is not finished; it is continually being improved with bug fixes and new features.
