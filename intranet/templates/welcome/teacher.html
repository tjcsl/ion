How to Navigate

Intranet is fully optimized for small screens. On your phone, tablet, or other mobile device, simply visit ion.tjhsst.edu. To access navigation items and the search bar, press the icon on the top left of the page next to the Intranet logo.

For detailed instructions on using the Ion teacher interface, press the Help button on the left-side navigation, or ask 8th Period Office Coordinator Catherine Massar.
