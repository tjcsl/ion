Advanced Search Tips

Searchable Fields

  • name
  • first
  • last
  • middle
  • nickname
  • id (e.g. 31863)
  • username (e.g. 2015elowman)
  • gradyear (e.g. 2015)
  • grade (9-12, staff)
  • sex
  • email

Examples

  • To find all members of the class of 2016:
    gradyear:2016
  • To find all females:
    sex:female
  • To find all males in the class of 2016:
    gradyear:2016 sex:male
  • To find senior girls with first names containing "an" followed by "a":
    first:an*a sex:female grade:12
  • To find users with a last name of "Le" or "Lee":
    last:le OR last:lee
  • To find teachers with last names ending in "y":
    grade:staff last:*y
